# Contact Rate

**Best table to use:** `support_de.metrics.unified_contact_rate`

## Definition

- **Numerator:** Bot + advocate handled conversations on a rolling 30-day basis
- **Denominator:** Transactions on a 30-day basis
  - Square transactions
  - Cash money movements
  - Afterpay orders *(to be added later — not yet in the table)*

## Scaling

The metric is expressed as **contacts per 1,000 transactions** — the raw ratio (numerator / denominator) is multiplied by 1,000 to produce the final value.
